After the fall...

By
Nick DeLena
on August 25, 2003 11:54 AM | Permalink | Comments (5)

This past Zaterdag was TOMFEST, 2K3: The Becoming (Drunk). I have to say it was an exhaustively enjoyable party. We tapped the keg around 2:30pm. I passed out face down on a mattress randomly strewn in a hallway around 5am.

There were lots of highlights. The Praetorians defeated FBI, someone committed two counts of haticide, Joe C's nickname changed from "Place Ta Be" to "Man Who Destroys That Which Is Sacred", our complete ransacking of Tom's fridge for food - from Tom we consumed "1 keg, 3 cases of beer, one 1.75 liter bottle of cap'n morgans spiced rum, one liter bottle of smirnoff rasberry vodka, half bottle of bacardi limon, random bottles of 40 oz smirnoff ice, and something about 4 budweisers, about 4 moretti's, about 2 peroni's and around 6 corona lights."

One rather peculiar moment was when a certain friend of a friend seemed to disapprove of my jocular Latin outburst. This girl stated: "Nick, you're a wicked cute kid, stop speaking Latin, maybe you'll get laid more." and later on - "You're insanely cute, but you're too uptight."

Am I uptight? I don't think so. Maybe she meant I was uptight because SOMEBODY THREW MY GODDAMN HAT IN THE POOL.

Other than that I think I was quite jovial the entire evening.

The next morning was complete and utter comedy. Around 8:45 I awoke to Melissa kicking me in the buttox region repeatedly. I was able to utter a "rrhrrrgh" noise before I fell back alseep. Minutes later both Jim and Melissa were kicking me to get up. I had pretty much the worst headache I've had in a long time. I think we cleaned out Tom's advil supply. Gillian joined us a few seconds later and we sat around Tom's kitchen trying to speak.

The ride back to Boston was tough for me - it took me a good 25 minutes to be able to regain my ability to speak lucidly. I was trying to give Jim directions in some kind of pidgin English. "You got back the round car other way" - meant "Turn around, you want to be going the other way."

It was absolutely beautiful yesterday. I opened all the windows in the apartment and just laid in bed all day. Beacon Hill was so quiet and breezy, it was great afternoon nap weather. I was so out of comission yesterday I went to bed at 11pm and got up this morning at around 6. I feel so rested.

Comments? Questions? Contact your local Nick DeLena representative.
