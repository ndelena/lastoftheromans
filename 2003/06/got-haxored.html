got haxored?

By
Nick DeLena
on June 20, 2003 6:43 PM | Permalink | Comments (0) | TrackBacks (0)

alright. someone hacked into my webserver yesterday. and you know, at first i was really annoyed that someone would do that, but they didn't do any damage, and they made me realize a huge (HUGE) loophole i left open, and i fixed it.

that having been said, i'm going to do a huge redesign of infinitelimitation.org. i've had the current design for a year and a half. i might as well do something productive and make it a little more artwanky. it used to be more artistic looking, then it sorta grew this techie face.

i will bring back the artfuck sensibility.

for the past two days i've been trying to write this big grandiose entry on my trip to new york city. it would be really witty and have these neat little paragraph titles. well i've lost my patience a
nd reverted to drinking vodka instead of finishing it each time. maybe you'll see it, maybe you wont. i had a good time though.

in other news, there is no other news. i might hold a geeked out party to celebrate my reaching the 5,000 mp3 mark. (5,046 exactly.)

i'm going for a jog along the charles now.
