job-related updates

By
Nick DeLena
on July 21, 2003 7:12 PM | Permalink | Comments (9)

After four years of intern slavery, I believe my day of emancipation may be at hand.

I had a brief meeting today with my boss. From what she says there is a written proposal in the works to get me hired effective August 25th as an hourly contractor until they can find me a full-time salaried position. I've known of this plan for a while, but got more specifics today: she "intends" to give me about double what my hourly pay is right now. She feels by January I stand a good chance of being converted into a salaried position.

After hearing this news, I nearly lost control of my facilities.

I think this would be quite a positive move in the short-term.


w00t.
