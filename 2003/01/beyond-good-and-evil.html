beyond good and evil

By
Nick DeLena
on January 5, 2003 11:58 PM | Permalink | Comments (0) | TrackBacks (0)

i think that the whole romantic concept of humanity being born helpless has been played out.

i have my new espresso machine. i am ready to make espresso + creme delicious enjoyable beverages, but nobody will drink them with me.

back to work tomorrow.

I can't help the feeling
I could blow through the ceiling
If I just turn and run
