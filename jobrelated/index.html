reasons to doubt

By
Nick DeLena
on October 24, 2003 7:04 PM | Permalink | Comments (0)

I received disturbing news regarding my job today which has led me slowly into a malaise. Just as I thought things were finally certain they are in doubt. Our reorganization into another group has once again come under the knife and I am being told to contact human resources for advice, as nothing is particularly certain. I wonder if they're telling me that my job is disappearing. This is substantially frustrating.

For a company I have worked for a total of four and a half years, I certainly have gotten little but mistreatment in the past several months. I shouldn't be surprised; I'm only a contribution to overall productivity, not a person.

Enough of that. I hate complaining, anyway. I will resolve this in one manner or another; be it a new role at the company or another job somewhere else.

In Cetera news, we are approaching our Nov. 1 submission deadline. We have several pieces in so far and things are looking good. I am really grateful that people have shown so much interest. Our plans are pretty much to produce the first issue on our own and then try to solicit advertisers. We have yet to discuss whether we will charge for the magazine. Of course we'd rather not, but printing costs will be a small fortune. I really wish I could focus on this full-time (and be able to survive financially). Perhaps some day.
