Swift Boat "Veterans" For Xenophobia and Ignorance

By
Nick DeLena
on August 16, 2004 5:27 PM | Permalink | Comments (7) | TrackBacks (0)

This one is unbelievable. As has been well publicized, the group Swift Boat Veterans for Truth has launched a large campaign to discredit John Kerry's military service. To compliment the television attack, they've now released a book titled "Unfit for Command." It is officially authored by two people: John E. O'Neill and Jerome R. Corsi. Media Matters, a watchdog group, recently did some groundbreaking investigation into exactly who this Jerome Corsi is, and the results are at first repulsive, but ultimately not surprising:


CORSI: Just don't let anybody put a tablet with the Ten Commandments in front of the school where that girl wants to wear a Muslim scarf -- OH, No --- then the RATS would complain. Anti-Christian, Anti-American -- just like their Presidential Candidate -- Jean Francois Kerrie.

CORSI: After he married TerRAHsa, didn't John Kerry begin practicing Judiasm? He also has paternal gradparents that were Jewish. What religion is John Kerry?

CORSI: John Fucking Commie Kerry and Commie Ted [Kennedy] discuss their plan to hand America over to our nation's enemies.

CORSI: Did the Journalist see Chubbie Chelsea among the wives. Little Katie Communist [Katie Couric] on the NBC Today show interviewed Hillary this morning and mom is worried sick about Chelsea. She was last seen in Kandahar at a Starbucks. But now, as Little Katie Communist sighed, "Who Knows?" Even British disinformation planted reports such as this grocery crap will be useful. Anyone with information about Chubbie Chelsea's whereabouts should post it now. Mom wants to know her daughter is out of harms way. Mom also wants to be at the center of the story.

CORSI: COMMUNISM -- it's simple NBC = NOTHING BUT COMMUNISM.

CORSI: Time to FREE Chris Matthews of MSNBC. MSNBC is beginning to stand for "More Shit, Nothing But Communism."

CORSI: Islam is like a virus --
it affects the mind -- maybe even better as an analogy -- it is a cancer that destroys the body it infects... No doctor would hesitate to eliminate cancer cells from the body.

So here we see an example of the kind of characters running the right-wing smear campaign. What gets me is this guy has a Ph.D. Couldn't he write a little more eloquently? At least make his unsubstantiated hatecrimes a little more artful. And lastly, if this guy thinks NBC is "Nothing But Communism" - what does that say in relation to his own political leaning?

More info at: http://mediamatters.org/items/200408060010
