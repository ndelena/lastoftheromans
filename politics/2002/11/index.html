one of the reasons i like massachusetts so much

By
Nick DeLena
on November 20, 2002 11:41 PM | Permalink | Comments (0) | TrackBacks (0)

the following is a clip from a cartoon called "Get Your War On!" which appears in the Editorial Humor publication, based out of Allston. it's gold. i will now quote.

-----------------------

Person1: "Hey, are you on CNN.com? They've got a really interesting poll; they ask 'Is al-Qaeda sending coded messages to followers via video statements?' You can answer 'Yes' or 'No'!"

Person2: "What about 'How the fuck would I know?' Who's qualified to answer a goddamn poll about coded video statements? Any American who bothers to answer that poll probably masturbates to Tom Clancy novels!"

Person1: "Wow, 100,000 responses!"

-----------------------------------

Person1: "If you could say one thing to god right now, what would it be?"

Person2: "I think I would say, 'Thank you, God, for your healing gift of religion.' What about you?"

Person1: "I'd say, 'God, I regret to inform You that U.S. policy now dictates we bomb the fuck out of You up in Heaven."

Person2: "Oh, I'd also say, 'Monotheistic religion has always brought out the best in us humans; thank you so much for the idea of a vengeful supernatural entity who rewards people in the afterlife! That shit makes a lot of sense!"
------------------------------------

gold.

By
Nick DeLena
on November 6, 2002 10:51 AM | Permalink | Comments (0) | TrackBacks (0)

this is the worst day in politics i can ever reme
mber.

Mitt Romney is not my governnor!

i saw a film clip of Mitt Romney's father giving a speech when he was governor of Michigan. the clip was black and white with no sound. it looked like a 1938 nuremberg rally.

it's this kind of shit (nationwide i might add) that will probably finally motivate me to join a political campaign.... jill stein in.... 2006? yikes. i'll be 26!

this is awful.


this government does not speak for me.
