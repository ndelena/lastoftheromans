recap

By
Nick DeLena
on November 19, 2002 6:27 AM | Permalink | Comments (0) | TrackBacks (0)

i have been so busy the past few days, that i have had no time to update this.

in summary:

Thursday: i finished my last day of work at adventis, spent a few hours at the suffolk journal office, and went back to the apartment to clean. alison came thursday night by bus, and surprisingly it was on time. we grabbed some food at the pour house on boylston.

Friday: after sleeping a million hours, we ventured up to UNH, which is always a solid time. jon was pretty restrained, he didn't poop in any girls hands, at least that we know of. kevin somehow thought jon was on fire and blasted his fire extinguisher around, coating jon, and his own apartment in a very september 11th-like brownish dust. it tasted like soap.

during the course of the night i had somewhere in the range of 9 beers and a majority stake in a champagne bottle.

they got me a miniature air horn (read: HURRRRRNNNN) for my birthday. score. i plan to make its debut on the T this week. not quite sure how yet though.

saturday we headed back after having breakfast at the friendly toast in portsmouth. we got back and took a nap. i attempted to finish my article for this week's journal, but writers block smacked me in the arse. later, alison treated me to a wonderful birthday dinner at the bay tower room. i rocked the suit.

Sunday: MY 22nd BIRTHDAY(!) we got up and had some eats at au bon pain, then did a little shopping at the galleria. alison took a 3:30 bus back to ct. i headed to revere where my family was having a little birthday party for me. i did the family thing, then bought a few things for the apartment, like a lamp for the living room, a lemon candle for the living room, and got my grandmother's old microwave. i'm trying to make the living room more 'livable'. without cable tv, we don't really use it that much, unless people are over. which is too bad, because its huge.

i'm too tired to put anything else.
