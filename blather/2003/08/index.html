Inspirat Inspirarat

By
Nick DeLena
on August 19, 2003 11:10 PM | Permalink | Comments (2)

I was having a conversation with a friend the other day when the word "familial" was used. For some reason I thought of "fambrosial." I think that's the funniest concept I've not laughed about in a long time.

>insert big dumb instant messenger face here<

I have been impoverished of late; after paying off my credit card debt a bit prematurely, I have been living check to check. My rent expense is monumental. But when I break down my personal finances - I should be doing fine. Or at least better than I am. I've paid $6,034.94 in taxes this year. That seems like a lot to me, but it only represents about a 26.2% tax rate. So I'm not even being over-taxed. I'm just spending my money like a bastard. This is depressing.

I haven't had a raise in two years.

I'm still waiting for this job upgrade.

I hate this money bullshit.
