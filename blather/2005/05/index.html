French-Canadian Software Humor

By
Nick DeLena
on May 16, 2005 8:52 AM | Permalink | Comments (0) | TrackBacks (0)

At City Hall we use a program called Munis developed by a French Canadian company that handles everything from payroll to accounts payable. This morning I was changing my password when I noticed an option for the program to generate a password for you that's supposed to be easily pronouncable. In light of this (and in light of the new Star Wars movie coming out Thursday) I have pasted the first several generated passwords. Let me know if you think they're easily pronounced - or if they bear any resemblance to Star Wars names...
