The Ides of March

By
Nick DeLena
on March 15, 2005 7:04 PM | Permalink | Comments (1) | TrackBacks (0)

Amusing instant messenger conversation:

[15:37] Jim Cormier: Beware, Nicholas, beware
[15:38] Jim Cormier: A soothsayer bids you beware the ides of March.
[15:40] Nick DeLena: What man is that?
[15:40] Nick DeLena: Set him before me; let me see his face.
[15:41] Nick DeLena: Fellow, come from the throng, look upon Delenicus.
